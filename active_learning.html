Active Learning

I have found project based or active learning to be an extremely effective teaching strategy. Some of the potential benefits include:

  • Increases student interest & engagement and helps with self-organization
  • Helps students develop creativity, patience with problem solving, and collaboration skills - all needed in today's workplace and world
  • Helps students learn content material in a more meaningful way
  • Allows teachers to teach in a more satisfying way
