Active Learning

Project based or active learning can be an extremely effective teaching strategy. Some of the potential benefits include:

  • Motivates students
  • Prepares students for college, careers, and citizenship
  • Helps students meet standards and do well on tests that ask students to demonstrate in-depth knowledge and thinking skills
  • Allows teachers to teach in a more satisfying way
  • Provides schools and districts with new ways to communicate and connect with parents, communities, and the wider world.1
