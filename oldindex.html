About Me

I've been an educator for more years than I can count.

I started becoming more invested in using technology for teaching when I discovered that I could use it to better serve my adult GED learners who often had to practice math via video chat in the evenings after they put their children to bed.

The more doors I could see opening for my students because of effective technology tools, the more I wanted to learn.

During the COVID 19 pandemic I continued to find new ways to help my elementary ESOL students access content and community in a virtual way.

At Home

I'm originally from New Hampshire, but came to Virginia for school, fell in love, and never left. My husband and I have raised our three sons in Charlottesville. I enjoy playing and coaching soccer, dancing salsa and weekend bagels and disc golf with my family.
