About Me

I've been an educator for more years than I can count.

Successful education outcomes with effective technology tools, made me want to learn more. Using video chat, I have taught adult GED students math remotely after they put their children to bed and I was able to support my elementary English learners virtually throughout the COVID pandemic.

The MSET program helped me continue to learn ways to integrate technology effectively and deepen learning experiences for my students.

At Home

I'm originally from New Hampshire, but came to Virginia for school, fell in love, and never left. My husband and I have raised our three sons in Charlottesville. I enjoy playing and coaching soccer, dancing salsa and weekend bagels and disc golf with my family.
