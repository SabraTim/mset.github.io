Accessible Design Theme

In Dr. Smith's Multimedia Production class, we learned how a color blind visitor might struggle viewing and reading some web sites.

The more I started paying attention, the more I wanted to understand about designing to help make content available for those with dyslexia, low vision, and physical challenges. Working with students who are new to English also gives me an appreciation of how powerful images can be in communicating information. I still have a lot to learn.
